About MaPacare

Welcome to MapaCare, where we are dedicated to connecting caregivers with elderly individuals in need of assistance. Our mission is to improve the quality of life for seniors by providing compassionate and reliable care tailored to their unique needs and preferences.

Our Mission

Empower caregivers to provide high-quality care to elderly individuals. Enhance the well-being and independence of seniors by matching them with compatible caregivers. Foster meaningful connections and relationships between caregivers and care recipients.

Our Approach

We believe in a personalized approach to caregiving, where each elderly individual's needs and preferences are carefully considered and respected. Our innovative matching algorithm ensures that caregivers are paired with seniors based on compatibility, skills, and shared interests, leading to more fulfilling caregiving experiences for both parties.

Get Involved

Services

Bathing

Caregivers assist the elderly person with various aspects of bathing, including getting into and out of the bathtub or shower safely. They may provide physical support, guidance, and reassurance throughout the bathing process.

Feeding

Prepare nutritious meals that align with the elderly person's dietary needs, preferences, and any restrictions. Assist with feeding if the individual has difficulty feeding themselves due to physical limitations or cognitive impairment.

Cooking

Caregivers prepare meals in accordance with the planned menu, taking into account the elderly person's taste preferences, cultural preferences, and dietary requirements. They ensure that meals are cooked safely and hygienically.

Transporting

For individuals who use wheelchairs, there may be specialized wheelchair transport services or individuals trained in transporting individuals in wheelchairs safely and comfortably.
